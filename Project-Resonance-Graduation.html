Rulebook

Guide your two friends through 28 days of senior year. Balance Joy, Stress, Career, and Friendship Bond.

🎴 Cards & Mastery

Start with 10 cards. Draw 5 each morning (mulligan up to 2). Play cards during Evening. Cards gain Mastery XP per play — Level 2 at 30 XP (+20% effects), Level 3 at 80 XP (unlock bonuses).

⏳ Time Crystals

You have 3💎/day (Casual: 4, Nightmare: 2). Morning events consume some. Spend the rest on cards.

✨ Critical Hits

Every card has a 10% chance to CRIT for 1.5× effects. Relics can boost this.

🎯 Momentum Streak

End days with both players' Joy > 40 to build streak. At 3+: +1 gold/day. At 5+: +1 card draw/day.

🤝 Friendship Bond

Bond decays by 2 each day. Keep it high — low bond blocks powerful synergy cards. Above 80 = "Unbreakable".

👹 Boss Days

Days 7, 14, 21, 28 are Boss Days with high-stakes events (finals, thesis defense, etc.).

🛒 Shop & Relics

Every 3 days: shop (buy cards, remove weak ones, upgrade cards). Every 7 days: draft a free Relic.

🏆 Endings

  • Burnout: Joy ≤ 0 → Failed run
  • Early Offer: Both Careers ≥ 100 → Speedrun win
  • Graduation: Day 28 → Graded ending (F to S+)
